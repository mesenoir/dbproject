Description

“School Database” is a Database Management System (DBMS) for school administration intended to remove some flaws of the traditional paper-based tasks and give a solution for database-related problems. Specifically, the current DBMS’s target is public schools in rural areas of Uzbekistan, where school administration needs a simple system to complete simple operations over students’ data.

As many cities in Uzbekistan have problems related to electricity and Internet connection, public schools are unable to access or use some online tools like Google classroom, TalentCard, Moodle, and many others. First Uzbek online school-platform Kundalik.com is also considered to be complex for users. The main problem is not only the Internet or electricity but teachers’ and administrations’ low rate of IT literacy. Systems with many features and complex structures create confusion among teachers and it is reflected in both teachers' and students’ performance.
